Leather Tack

We do our best to match leather and thread color, corners are eased and edges dyed and polished. From replacing a keeper on an English headstall to replacing a jockey on a saddle we’ll do our best to make your tack look as good as new.

Nylon Tack

Nylon tack is also repairable, though many won’t mess with it. We can replace broken straps, buckles, and ends!

Custom Fitting

Do you have a halter that is too tight around the nose or too long in the throat latch? We can fix that!
