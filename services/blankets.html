We realize that your horse blankets are an investment in the care and overall health of your horse and take the utmost care in providing the best and most affordable cleaning service around. Often times repairing a horse blanket is more cost effective then purchasing a new one. Many damaged blankets may appear beyond repair, but in most cases the repairs are fairly straight forward. If you’re concerned with cost, simply ask for free estimate!

Wash & Waterproof

Detergents are never used on your blankets and sheets and we only use products made specifically for horse blankets. If necessary, excessively dirty blankets will be hand brushed and scrubbed prior to being washed*. Blankets are then washed in warm water with Nikwax Rug Wash, and then hung to air dry in the sun. All blankets must be washed prior to being repaired or waterproofed. (Please feel free to launder them yourself, but please note they must meet our specifications before we can put them on our sewing machine.) After thoroughly drying, your blanket may be air-tumbled to remove the last of the loose horse hair.

Mend & Repair

After laundering, your blanket is taken into the shop and repaired as needed. We completely inspect all seams and bindings, verify the lining is in good shape, and all leg, belly straps, and d-rings are securely in place. All tears are professionally repaired, and if needed, filling is replaced in heavier blankets. After repairs, at your request, blankets are waterproofed using Nikwax Rug Proof. Rug Proof is specifically designed for waterproofing horse blankets and is recommended by Rambo Blankets. After waterproofing the blanket is hung and allowed to air-dry. Once ready to go your blanket is folded and placed in a clear plastic bag ready to be returned!

We take pride in matching fabric and colors as close as possible and will never charge more for a repair than the blanket is worth. If questionable we will always look on line to see what a comparable blanket would cost. If it appears that the repair will be costly we will always contact you first for your approval.

Rug Wash

Safely revitalises insulation and water repellency. If a breathable waterproof rug loses its water repellency, it will suffer a severe reduction in its ability to breathe. This will lead to a build up of condensation on the inside of the rug so the horse will become clammy, cold and uncomfortable. Always use Nikwax Rug Wash for cleaning breathable waterproof horse rugs such as those made by Rambo.

Benefits of using this product:

  • It prolongs the life of horse rugs and optimises performance
  • It maintains breathability
  • It maintains original water-repellency
  • It’s easy to apply - can be used in a domestic or commerical washing machine
  • Suitable for use on all rugs -, including those with breathable membranes
  • It lifts out dirt that can attract water and mask water repellent finishes
  • Unlike conventional detergents, it does not leave behind water attractive residues which encourage ‘wetting-out’
  • It will remove detergent residues left behind by previous washes
  • Water based, environmentally friendly, biodegradable, non-flammable, non-hazardous
  • Does not contain fluorocarbons

Rug Proof

  • Adds / renews water repellency and revives breathability of horse rugs
  • Resists water absorption due to flex & abrasion damage
  • Prolongs the life of gear and optimises performance in wet weather
  • Maintains insulation, keeping your horse warm
  • Treatment will reduce the growth of mildew in the rug
  • Easy to apply - can be used in a washing machine or applied by hand, no tumble drying required
  • Will not affect cosmetic appearance
  • Water based, environmentally friendly, biodegradable, non-flammable, non-hazardous
  • Does not contain fluorocarbons
